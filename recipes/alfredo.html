Chicken Fettucine Alfredo

Need a quick and filling dinner to impress someone with? Try out the recipe and I can guarantee you will be satisfied!

Ingredients!

  • 32oz of heavy whipping cream
  • 1lb of parmesan
  • 1lb of mozzarella
  • 2 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 2lbs of boneless chicken breast
  • 1 lb of Fettucine

Cooking Instructions!

  1. Cut up chicken breasts into small cube shaped pieces (size may vary)
  2. Grab a pan to cook chicken in and cook until golden brown
  3. Put chicken aside and prepare for alfredo sauce
  4. In a clean pan bring heavy whipping cream to a simmer
  5. Add in all sauce ingredients
  6. Once cheese is fully melted, add chicken to sauce and keep on low heat
  7. Boil your fettucine noodles
  8. Once noodles are fully cooked add to your sauce and serve
