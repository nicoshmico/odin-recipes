Mac'n'Cheese

Impress all of your friends and family by bringing the worlds best macaroni and cheese to this year's Thanksgiving!

Ingredients!

  • 2 lbs of elbow macaroni
  • 32oz of heavy whipping cream
  • 16oz of sharp cheddar
  • 16oz of medium cheddar
  • 16oz of mild cheddar
  • 8oz of gouda
  • 8oz of monterrey jack
  • 1 block of velveeta

Cooking Instructions!

  1. Shred your cheeses. Leave about 8oz to top the dish once everything is in the baking pan.
  2. In a pan, bring heavy whipping cream to a slight simmer.
  3. Start adding cheeses slowly until all of the cheese is melted in pan.
  4. Bring a pot of water to a boil and then add noodles.
  5. After noodles finish cooking, add to a baking pan and spread the cheese sauce all over and mix it well.
  6. Place in oven covered with aluminum foil for about 30 minutes then take off foil and leave in for 15 minutes for a cripier crust.
  7. Once out of the oven let cool for about 5 minutes then it is ready to be served!
